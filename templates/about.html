Year Founded

2025

Ever felt frustrated by cluttered video downloaders riddled with ads and privacy concerns? So did we. That's why in 2025, we set out to build Fetch-Url – a different kind of tool. We envisioned a seamless, private, and lightning-fast experience, putting you in control of the media you love without the noise and distractions. Our mission isn't just about downloading videos; it's about reclaiming your time and ensuring a smooth, secure experience every single time.

Our small but passionate team blends technology with usability — no clutter, no ads, no tracking. Just a simple tool that works.
