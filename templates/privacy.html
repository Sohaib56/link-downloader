Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We’re just here to help you download, anonymously.

At Fetch-Url, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data – or, more accurately, how we don't.

We do not store your video URLs or personal data.

When you use Fetch-Url, the video URLs you interact with are processed transiently within your browser. We do not log, save, or retain these URLs or any other personally identifiable information on our servers.

We do not track user activity or require registration.

You can use Fetch-Url anonymously. We do not require any account creation, and we do not employ any methods to track your browsing history or usage patterns beyond aggregated, privacy-respecting analytics (as mentioned below).

All downloads are processed client-side in your browser.

The process of fetching and preparing the video for download happens directly within your web browser. This means the video data does not pass through our servers, further ensuring your privacy.

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, cookie-free solution.

To understand how our website is used in a general sense and to help us improve it, we utilize Plausible Analytics. This is a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect any personal data. The information we gather is aggregated and anonymous, providing insights into website traffic without identifying individual users.

We are always happy to address any concerns you may have regarding your privacy. support@fetch-url.com.
